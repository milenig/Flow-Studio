Questions and answers

Frequently asked questions

Can I know the price in advance?

Yes. Before kickoff, we define scope and price clearly: fixed quote, written timelines, and no hidden add-ons at the end.

How long does a website project usually take?

Most standard scopes are delivered in around two weeks, with clear checkpoints and concise revision rounds.

Can I edit website content myself?

Yes. We set up CMS so you can update blog posts, team members, portfolio items, and most sections without developer support.

Is SEO included from the start?

Yes. Semantic HTML, structured data where relevant, and fast loading are part of our technical baseline from day one.

Can AI agents and automation be integrated?

Absolutely. Where it makes sense, we integrate AI tools, AI agents, and automation (e.g. Make or n8n) to reduce manual tasks and connect your site with internal workflows.

What happens after launch - do you offer support?

We stay available for 30 days after launch. Ongoing maintenance and larger updates can be arranged through a separate support plan.
